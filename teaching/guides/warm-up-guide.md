# Badminton Warm-up Guide: Preparing Your Body for Play

## Why Warm-up Matters
A proper warm-up increases blood flow, raises muscle temperature, and prepares your joints for the explosive, multi-directional movements of badminton. Skipping warm-up significantly increases injury risk, especially for the ankles, knees, shoulders, and wrists.

**Recommended Duration**: 10-15 minutes before play.

---

## Phase 1: Pulse Raiser (3-5 Minutes)
*Goal: Elevate heart rate and increase blood flow.*

| Exercise | Duration | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Light Jogging | 2 min | Around the court or in place |
| High Knees | 30 sec | Drive knees upward, pump arms |
| Butt Kicks | 30 sec | Heels touch glutes |
| Side Shuffles | 30 sec each direction | Stay low, athletic stance |
| Skipping | 1 min | Optional, great for coordination |

---

## Phase 2: Joint Mobilization (3-4 Minutes)
*Goal: Lubricate joints and increase range of motion.*

### Upper Body
*   **Neck Mobility**: gently look left/right, and tilt ear to shoulder. **Avoid full circle rotations** to protect the cervical spine.
*   **Shoulder Circles**: Forward and backward (15 each direction).
*   **Arm Swings**: Cross-body horizontal swings (20 reps).
*   **Wrist Rotations**: Interlace fingers and rotate wrists (20 each direction). **Critical for badminton!**
*   **Elbow Circles**: Hands on shoulders, draw circles with elbows (10 each direction).

### Lower Body
*   **Hip Circles**: Hands on hips, large circles (10 each direction).
*   **Leg Swings (Front-Back)**: Hold onto something for balance, swing one leg forward and back (15 each leg).
*   **Leg Swings (Side-to-Side)**: Swing leg across body (15 each leg).
*   **Knee Bends (Mini Squats)**: Feet shoulder-width, gently bend knees as if sitting (15 reps). **Avoid grinding knee rotation**.
*   **Ankle Rotations**: Lift one foot and rotate the ankle (15 each direction, each foot).

---

## Phase 3: Dynamic Stretching (3-4 Minutes)
*Goal: Actively stretch muscles through movement, NOT static holds.*

| Exercise | Reps/Duration | Target Area |
|----------|---------------|-------------|
| Walking Lunges | 10 each leg | Quads, Hip Flexors, Glutes |
| Lateral Lunges | 8 each side | Inner Thighs (Adductors) |
| Inchworms | 6 reps | Hamstrings, Core, Shoulders |
| Leg Cradles | 8 each leg | Glutes, Hip Rotators |
| Torso Twists | 15 each side | Spine, Obliques |
| Arm Circles (Large) | 15 forward, 15 backward | Shoulders, Rotator Cuff |

---

## Phase 4: Badminton-Specific Activation (2-3 Minutes)
*Goal: Mimic badminton movements to prime the nervous system.*

*   **Shadow Footwork**: Move to 4 corners without a shuttle (front-right, front-left, back-right, back-left). Focus on split step and recovery.
*   **Shadow Swings**: Practice forehand and backhand swings without a racket, focusing on rotation.
*   **Light Rally (if partner available)**: Slow-paced rallies focusing on touch and movement, NOT power. Clears and drops only.

---

## Quick Reference Checklist
- [ ] Pulse elevated (light sweat)
- [ ] All major joints mobilized (ankles, knees, hips, shoulders, wrists)
- [ ] Dynamic stretches completed (no static holds)
- [ ] Badminton movements rehearsed (footwork, swings)

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
1.  **Static stretching before playing**: Save static stretches for AFTER play. Dynamic stretches are for warm-up.
2.  **Rushing through it**: A 2-minute jog is NOT a warm-up. Take the full 10-15 minutes.
3.  **Ignoring wrists and ankles**: These are the most vulnerable joints in badminton.
4.  **Going 100% immediately**: The first few rallies should be slow and controlled.

# 羽毛球热身指南：为运动做好身体准备

## 为什么热身很重要
正确的热身可以增加血液流动、提升肌肉温度，并使关节为羽毛球运动中爆发性的多方向移动做好准备。跳过热身会显著增加受伤风险，尤其是脚踝、膝盖、肩膀和手腕。

**建议时长**：运动前 10-15 分钟。

---

## 第一阶段：心率提升 (3-5 分钟)
*目标：提升心率，增加血液流动。*

| 练习 | 时长 | 备注 |
|------|------|------|
| 慢跑 | 2 分钟 | 绕场或原地 |
| 高抬腿 | 30 秒 | 膝盖尽量抬高，摆臂配合 |
| 后踢腿 | 30 秒 | 脚后跟触碰臀部 |
| 侧向滑步 | 每侧 30 秒 | 保持低姿态，运动员站姿 |
| 跳绳 | 1 分钟 | 可选，有助于协调性 |

---

## 第二阶段：关节活动 (3-4 分钟)
*目标：润滑关节，增加活动范围。*

### 上半身
*   **颈部活动**：缓慢左右转头，侧头（耳贴肩）。**避免360度整圈旋转**以保护颈椎。
*   **肩部绕环**：向前和向后各 15 圈。
*   **手臂交叉摆动**：双臂在身前水平交叉摆动（20 次）。
*   **手腕绕环**：双手十指交叉，旋转手腕（每个方向 20 圈）。**羽毛球运动的关键！**
*   **肘部绕环**：双手搭肩，用肘部画圈（每个方向 10 圈）。

### 下半身
*   **髋部绕环**：双手叉腰，大幅度画圈（每个方向 10 圈）。
*   **前后摆腿**：扶住支撑物保持平衡，一腿前后摆动（每条腿 15 次）。
*   **左右摆腿**：腿部横向摆动（每条腿 15 次）。
*   **膝部屈伸（微蹲）**：双脚分立，像坐椅子一样轻微下蹲（15 次）。**避免膝盖负重绕环**。
*   **脚踝绕环**：抬起一只脚，旋转脚踝（每个方向、每只脚 15 圈）。

---

## 第三阶段：动态拉伸 (3-4 分钟)
*目标：通过动作主动拉伸肌肉，而非静态保持。*

| 练习 | 次数/时长 | 目标部位 |
|------|-----------|----------|
| 行进弓步 | 每腿 10 次 | 股四头肌、髋屈肌、臀肌 |
| 侧弓步 | 每侧 8 次 | 大腿内侧（内收肌） |
| 毛毛虫爬行 | 6 次 | 腘绳肌、核心、肩膀 |
| 抱膝抬腿 | 每腿 8 次 | 臀肌、髋关节旋转肌群 |
| 躯干扭转 | 每侧 15 次 | 脊柱、腹斜肌 |
| 大臂绕环 | 向前 15 圈，向后 15 圈 | 肩膀、肩袖肌群 |

---

## 第四阶段：羽毛球专项激活 (2-3 分钟)
*目标：模拟羽毛球动作，激活神经系统。*

*   **无球步伐练习**：无球状态下移动到四个角落（右前、左前、右后、左后）。重点关注启动步和回中。
*   **挥空拍练习**：不用球拍练习正手和反手挥拍动作，注重转体。
*   **轻松对拉（如有搭档）**：慢节奏对拉，注重手感和移动，**而非力量**。仅限高远球和吊球。

---

## 快速检查清单
- [ ] 心率已提升（微微出汗）
- [ ] 所有主要关节已活动开（脚踝、膝盖、髋部、肩膀、手腕）
- [ ] 动态拉伸已完成（无静态保持）
- [ ] 羽毛球动作已演练（步伐、挥拍）

---

## 常见错误
1.  **运动前进行静态拉伸**：静态拉伸应留到运动结束后。热身应使用动态拉伸。
2.  **敷衍了事**：2 分钟慢跑不算热身。请完整进行 10-15 分钟。
3.  **忽略手腕和脚踝**：这是羽毛球运动中最容易受伤的关节。
4.  **一上来就全力**：开始的几个回合应该是慢速且可控的。
