# Badminton Training Session Structure for Adult Beginners (>30 Years Old)

## Overview
This plan outlines the structure of a standard 2-hour training session designed specifically for adult beginners. The focus is on injury prevention, grasping fundamental mechanics, and enjoying the sport.

## Session Breakdown

### 1. Warm-up & Activation (15 Minutes)
*   **Goal**: Prepare the body for physical activity and reduce the risk of injury.
*   **Activities**:
    *   Light jogging or skipping (pulse raiser).
    *   Dynamic stretching (arm circles, leg swings, torso twists).
    *   Wrist and ankle mobility exercises (critical for badminton).
    *   *Note*: Emphasize smooth movements, no sudden jerks.

### 2. Fundamental Technique Instruction (20 Minutes)
*   **Goal**: Introduce a specific skill (e.g., Forehand Grip & High Serve).
*   **Method**:
    *   Demonstrate the correct grip (V-shape).
    *   Show the correct swing path (without shuttle).
    *   Explain the "why" and "how" clearly.
    *   Players practice "shadow" swings while the coach corrects form.

### 3. Multi-shuttle Drills / Skill Practice (40 Minutes)
*   **Goal**: Build muscle memory through repetition.
*   **Activities**:
    *   **Static Drills**: Players stand in one spot; coach feeds shuttles to the specific area.
    *   **Focus**: Contact point, timing, and grip transition.
    *   **Rotation**: Rotate players frequently to ensure rest intervals suitable for the age group.

### 4. Footwork & Movement Basics (20 Minutes)
*   **Goal**: Learn how to move efficiently to reach the shuttle.
*   **Activities**:
    *   Chasse step (side-to-side).
    *   Lunging technique (protecting the knee).
    *   Shadow footwork to the 4 corners (slow pace).

### 5. Fun Games / Controlled Sparring (15 Minutes)
*   **Goal**: Apply skills in a low-pressure environment and have fun.
*   **Activities**:
    *   Half-court singles or cooperative rallies (aiming for longest rally).
    *   "King of the Court" (simplified rules).

### 6. Stretching & Debrief (10 Minutes)
*   **Goal**: Aid recovery and flexibility.
*   **Activities**:
    *   Static stretching (hamstrings, calves, shoulders, forearms).
    *   Debrief and Q&A session.

# 成人羽毛球初学者（30岁+）训练课程结构

## 概述
本计划概述了一节标准2小时训练课的结构，专为成人初学者设计。核心重点在于预防受伤、掌握基础动作力学以及享受羽毛球的乐趣。

## 环节拆解

### 1. 热身与激活 (15 分钟)
*   **目标**: 唤醒身体机能，降低受伤风险。
*   **活动**:
    *   慢跑或跳绳（提升心率）。
    *   动态拉伸（绕臂、摆腿、转体）。
    *   手腕和脚踝灵活性练习（羽毛球运动的关键）。
    *   *注意*: 强调动作平滑，避免突然发力。

### 2. 基础技术教学 (20 分钟)
*   **目标**: 引入一项特定技术（例如：正手握拍与高远球发球）。
*   **方法**:
    *   演示正确握拍（V字型）。
    *   展示正确挥拍轨迹（无球状态）。
    *   清晰解释动作原理。
    *   学员进行“挥空拍”练习，教练纠正姿势。

### 3. 多球训练 / 技能练习 (40 分钟)
*   **目标**: 通过重复建立肌肉记忆。
*   **活动**:
    *   **原地定点训练**: 学员站在固定位置，教练喂球至特定落点。
    *   **重点**: 击球点、击球时机以及握拍切换。
    *   **轮换**: 频繁轮换学员，确保适合该年龄段的休息间隔。

### 4. 步伐与基本移动 (20 分钟)
*   **目标**: 学习如何高效移动到位。
*   **活动**:
    *   并步移动 (Chasse step)。
    *   跨步技术（注意保护膝盖）。
    *   四点米字步（慢速练习）。

### 5. 趣味游戏 / 控制性对练 (15 分钟)
*   **目标**: 在低压环境下应用技能，增加乐趣。
*   **活动**:
    *   半场单打或合作多拍（目标是尽量多回合不失误）。
    *   “擂台赛” (简化规则)。

### 6. 拉伸与总结 (10 分钟)
*   **目标**: 帮助恢复，提升柔韧性。
*   **活动**:
    *   静态拉伸（大腿后侧、小腿、肩膀、前臂）。
    *   总结与问答环节。
