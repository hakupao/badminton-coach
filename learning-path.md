# Systematic Learning Path for Badminton Beginners

## Overview
This document outlines a progressive roadmap for a complete beginner to systematically learn badminton. It separates the learning curve into **Racket Skills (Strokes/Hands)** and **Footwork (Movement/Legs)**, as these two must develop in harmony.

---

## Phase 1: The Foundation (0 - 3 Months)
*Focus: Correct Habits & Safety*

### Racket Skills (The Hands)
1.  **Grip Mastery (The V-Grip)**:
    *   Neutral Grip (Shake-hands).
    *   Thumb Grip (Backhand).
    *   Transitioning between grips smoothly.
2.  **Net Play (Touch)**:
    *   Net Lift (Underhand clear) - Forehand & Backhand.
    *   Net Shot (Tumble/Spin) - Basic contact point.
3.  **Basic Service**:
    *   Low Serve (Backhand) - for Doubles/Singles.
    *   High Serve (Forehand) - for Singles mechanics mastery.

### Footwork (The Legs)
1.  **Ready Position**:
    *   Split step base.
    *   Center of gravity management.
2.  **Front Court Movement**:
    *   Lunge technique (Heel-Toe roll).
    *   Chasse step + Lunge.
    *   Recovery back to center.
3.  **Basic Split Step**:
    *   Timing the split step when the opponent hits the ball.

---

## Phase 2: Overhead Basics & Rear Court (3 - 6 Months)
*Focus: Rotation & Power Generation*

### Racket Skills (The Hands)
1.  **Overhead Strokes (Forehand)**:
    *   **High Clear (Defensive & Attacking)**: Essential for resetting rallies.
        *   Pronation (Forearm rotation).
        *   Point of contact (High and in front).
    *   **Drop Shot**: Same preparation as clear, but soft contact.
2.  **Drive**:
    *   Flat shots across the net (Mid-court urgency).
3.  **Smash (Basic)**:
    *   Standing smash (accuracy over power initially).

### Footwork (The Legs)
1.  **Rear Court Movement**:
    *   Scissor Kick (switching feet in air).
    *   Chasse step backward.
    *   China Jump (optional for advanced mobility).
2.  **Mid-Court Movement**:
    *   Side-to-side chasse steps for defense/drives.
3.  **Flow**:
    *   Linking front court and rear court movements smoothly.

---

## Phase 3: Advanced Skills & Tactics (6 - 12 Months)
*Focus: Precision, Deception & Speed*

### Racket Skills (The Hands)
1.  **Backhand Overhead (The difficult one)**:
    *   Backhand Clear.
    *   Backhand Drop.
2.  **Advanced Net Game**:
    *   Cross-court net shots.
    *   Net kill (Tap).
3.  **Deception**:
    *   Using the same preparation for Clear, Drop, and Smash.
    *   Slicing the shuttle (Reverse slice / Straight slice).

### Footwork (The Legs)
1.  **Full Court Coverage**:
    *   6-corner footwork drills.
2.  **Double Step / Hop Step**:
    *   Adjusting micro-positioning before striking.
3.  **Recovery Speed**:
    *   Explosive return to base.

# 羽毛球零基础系统学习路径

## 概述
本文档为羽毛球初学者提供了一条循序渐进的学习路线图。我们将学习过程分为**手法（手上技术）**和**步伐（脚下移动）**两条主线，这两者必须协同发展。

---

## 第一阶段： 夯实基础 (0 - 3 个月)
*核心：正确习惯与安全*

### 手法 (The Hands)
1.  **握拍精通 (V字握拍)**:
    *   中性握拍 (握手式/正手)。
    *   大拇指顶宽面 (反手)。
    *   握拍的流畅切换 (灵活的手指)。
2.  **网前技术 (球感)**:
    *   网前挑球 (下手高远球) - 正手与反手。
    *   网前放小球 (搓球) - 基础触球点练习。
3.  **基础发球**:
    *   反手发小球 - 双打/单打通用。
    *   正手发高球 - 掌握鞭打发力的原型。

### 步伐 (The Legs)
1.  **准备姿势**:
    *   启动步的基础站位。
    *   重心的控制。
2.  **前场移动**:
    *   跨步技术 (脚跟-脚尖滚动，膝盖不过脚尖)。
    *   并步 + 跨步。
    *   回动至中心位置。
3.  **基础启动步 (Split Step)**:
    *   学习在对手击球瞬间轻轻一跳。

---

## 第二阶段： 后场基础与发力 (3 - 6 个月)
*核心：侧身旋转与力量传导*

### 手法 (The Hands)
1.  **正手头顶技术**:
    *   **高远球 (防守与进攻)**: 摆脱被动的核心技术。
        *   内旋发力 (小臂内旋)。
        *   击球点 (最高点且在身前)。
    *   **吊球**: 动作一致性，但触球轻柔。
2.  **平抽挡**:
    *   中场的快节奏平球对抗。
3.  **杀球 (基础)**:
    *   原地杀球 (先追求落点精准，再追求力量)。

### 步伐 (The Legs)
1.  **后场移动**:
    *   剪刀跳/换步 (Scissor Kick - 空中交换双脚)。
    *   后退并步。
    *   马来步 (头顶区侧身大跨步)。
2.  **中场移动**:
    *   左右两侧的接杀球并步。
3.  **连贯性**:
    *   前后场步伐的流畅衔接。

---

## 第三阶段： 进阶与战术 (6 - 12 个月)
*核心：精准、欺骗性（一致性）与速度*

### 手法 (The Hands)
1.  **反手头顶区域 (难点)**:
    *   反手高远球 (被动过渡)。
    *   反手吊球。
2.  **进阶网前**:
    *   勾对角。
    *   扑球 (封网)。
3.  **假动作/一致性**:
    *   高远、吊、杀动作一致性 (让对手猜不出)。
    *   切球/滑板 (劈吊)。

### 步伐 (The Legs)
1.  **全场覆盖**:
    *   六点步伐综合训练。
2.  **调整步**:
    *   击球前的微小碎步调整。
3.  **回动速度**:
    *   爆发性回位。
